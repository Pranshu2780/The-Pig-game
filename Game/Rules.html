Game Rule

1. The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
2. In each turn, a player can roll a dice as many times as he wishes. Each result get added to his Round score.
3. But, if the palyer rolls a 1, all is Round score get lost. After that, it's the next palyer's turn.
4. The player can choose to 'hold', which means his Round score gets added to Global score. After that, it's the next player turn.
5. The first player to reach 100 points on Global score wins the game.
